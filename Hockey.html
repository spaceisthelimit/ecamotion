Yerba Buena Ice Skating & Bowling Center

"As San Francisco’s only year-round ice skating center, we are open for various programs on an average of 18 hours a day, 7 days a week. We offer a wide variety of regular programs in our schedule – fun and exciting opportunities include: Public Skating, Freestyle sessions for competitive skaters, Skating School for both figure skating and hockey skating at all levels of ability and ages, Private Instruction, Youth Hockey League, Adult Hockey League, Adult pick-up hockey, Skating sessions especially for adults and seniors, Synchronized Team skating for youth and adults and Brian Boitano’s Youth Skate Program. The rink is a standard NHL sized rink 80’x200’ with slightly rounded corners."

SF Sabercats

"The San Francisco Sabercats are dedicated to the development of young athletes through the sport of ice hockey. Our coaches are experienced, USA Hockey certified instructors with a passion for hockey development. "

CYO Athletics

CYO Athletics in the San Francisco Archdiocese serves Marin, north coastal San Mateo and San Francisco counties and offers Boy’s and Girl’s Soccer, Boy’s & Girl’s Basketball, and countless other sports. Additionally we have a Physical Education program that we offer. Currently, we serve over 11,000 kids in 3 counties and continue to build leaders every day
