Yerba Buena Center for the Arts


"YBCA welcomes the support of volunteers who want to give back to their community by supporting San Francisco’s premier venue for contemporary art. Currently, we are seeking individuals with experience in customer service to provide assistance to our staff and guests during events. Yerba Buena Center for the Arts (YBCA) is committed to diversity in its programming and in creating a work culture and environment that is reflective of San Francisco Bay Area demography. YBCA encourages and actively recruits applicants representing dimensions of difference that include — but are not limited to — age, national origin, ethnicity, race, religion, ability, sexual orientation, gender or political affiliation."(source)

The Mix at San Francisco Public Library


"A library space where teens ages 13-18 can explore books, music making, video production, robotics and much more in a welcoming and creative space! Events are held at the San Francisco Main Library. If you are interested in cooking, poetry, and arts, this is the right place for you!"(source)

Harvey Milk Photo Center


"The Harvey Milk Photo Center is the oldest and largest community darkroom west of the Mississippi, and is part of the San Francisco Recreation & Parks Department. We also offer a Digital Lab at our facility to our members. We have been in our current location since 1953. All exhibits, lectures, special events, memberships, are offered to the public. Our wide selection of classes and workshops are taught by working professionals within the industry, in both fine arts and commercial photography. And we offer scholarship programs for anyone interested in taking classes and workshops. HMPC is a non-profit agency." (source)
