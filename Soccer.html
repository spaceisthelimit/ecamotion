San Francisco Youth Soccer

SF Youth Soccer runs scheduling for the SFYS Recreational and competitive CCSL Prep leagues. We support teams in multiple non-SFYS leagues as well. Standings for SFYS Leagues impact seeding and playoffs in Fall and between seasons. In Spring, there are no playoffs but in SFYS Rec & CCSL Prep, season performance can be used as a basis for bracket placement in Fall.

San Francisco Vikings Soccer Club

The U8 - U11 Vikings Soccer Skills Academy is a developmental soccer program designed for and open to U8 - U11 San Francisco Youth Soccer League recreational players. The purpose of the Academy is to provide soccer players with a high quality training program. Training and curriculum is overseen by Jeff Wilson, Director of Coaching with the Vikings Travel Club. The Academy is designed for boys and girls who love soccer and are looking for developmental opportunities in addition to their practices with their fall team. The Academy is looking for players who are committed to developing their soccer skills and are mentally and emotionally ready to benefit from a structured technical training environment.

CYO Athletes

"Boys and girls 3rd-8th grade are eligible to play CYO soccer for a school or Parish sports program which participates in CYO Athletics. The criteria for eligibility is based on a child's residence or where they attend school. The child must either attend school or live within the geographic boundaries of the Catholic parish or program. Interested players should contact the Athletic Director for the specific school/program they wish to join."
