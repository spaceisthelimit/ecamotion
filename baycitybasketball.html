BAY CITY BASKETBALL

Basketball

Free try-outs and membership fee at no cost. Bay City Basketball is non-profit organization and provides financial aid for those who doesn't have the opportunity to participate in a professional sport.

Boys and girls within the age range of 8-18 are welcome to join. Or boys and girls from 3rd grade to 12th grade.

The season usually last from March to early August. Through Bay City, players will gain the skill of developing good sportsmanship and work together as a team.

Information

The Bay City Basketball program seeks to help youths who want to play basketball professionally along with personal growth and life skills.

For more info: http://www.baycitybasketball.com/
