Do you feel frustrated by the lack of extracurricular activities which match your needs? Ecamotion provides opportunities for teens,ranging from the ages of 14-18.

We strive to offer a variety of low-cost activities near you.

What categories we offer

Sports

Being on a sports helps you to build long lasting friendships and cheer each other up. Exercising can loosen up your mind and body. Team sports can help teens gain more confidence in what they do in life. Find local teams and games to participate and don’t forget to have spirit!

Art and Theatre

Do you love performing arts? From dancing to acting, we offer plenty of activities to keep you engaged. Expand your creativity and talent. Theatre brings everyone together, an opportunity to meet new people, and learn new skills.

STEM

Are you interested in math and science? Get a head start in the STEM field and experience what professionals. Not only that you will receive mentoring but also have a sense of what a STEM career path looks like. Almost everything involves technology the 21st century, so it’s important to obtain coding skills because STEM is relevant to our everyday lives.

Volunteer

Helping the community is a must because you are making an impact. Doing volunteer work can help you gain professional experience, promote personal growth, and expand your network. You can establish strong relationships and especially making a difference.

WHAT MAKES US GREAT

Easy to Use

Our product can easily be accessed on both desktop and mobile devices; the sleek interface allows users to maneuver effortlessly through the various activities Ecamotion has to offer.

By and For Students

Ecamotion is a website and organization run by Girls Who Code students. It is our mission to give back to the community using the CS skills we have learned throughout the GWC Summer Immersion Program.

Just for You

Ecamotion is catered to your every need and interest, whether that be centered around a variety of other activities. Teenagers are encouraged to indulge in their own world of endless possibilities.
