San Francisco Food Bank

Volunteer

"Tasks require sorting food package, assembling grocery boxes for seniors, and repackaging food into family sized quantities. The minimum age to volunteer is 8 years old and must be accompanied by an adult.

Currently looking for volunteers who are able to commit at least 30 hours in the CalFresh Program (must be at least 18 years old). Each event time requirement is usually 2-4 hours. Some duties/responsibilities include maintaining client confidentiality and doing some office work such as phone calls and screening data."

Information

"Every day of the week, teams of volunteers gather to sort and pack the food donations that come through our doors. From bins of oranges straight from the grove to 2,000-pound totes of rice, volunteers ensure that there are always hands at the ready to pack the food for distribution.
