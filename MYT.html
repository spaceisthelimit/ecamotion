Marsh Youth Theatre

Theatre

"MYT provides youth ages 2-21 with high quality theater arts experience that integrates drama, dance, music and stagecraft. Open to all young performers without audition or regard to financial limitations, MYT produces full-scale original musical productions each year for elementary, middle and high school/college performance ensembles.

Toddlers (2.5 yrs and up) – Toddler/Adult Flying Time! (Trapeze) – A creative playground for adults and toddlers while hanging around and spinning! Pre-K (up to 5 yrs old) – Emily Butterfly’s wonderful program for the little ones “STORYTELLING & DRAMATIC PLAY.”

Grades 3 through 8 – HIP HOP & WORLD DANCE with Rebecca Cervantes and Maica Folch’s always thrilling BAILANDO con TRAPECIOS (Trapeze).

Grades 4 through 8 – “TELL US YOUR STORY!” the oral tradition of storytelling with an emphasis on personal and family stories.

For our Teens – DIGITAL STORYTELLING & THE PERFORMANCE CONSERVATORY – This popular and exciting program focuses on developing the fundamentals of stage performance."

Information

"The Performance Conservatory will feature a series of two-hour “Master Classes” encompassing different aspects of the theater. Guest artists will include professionals in such areas as playwriting, stage combat, acting styles, script analysis, the audition process, improvisation, movement, music, masks, and stage craft."
