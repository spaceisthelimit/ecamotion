SPORTS: BASKETBALL

Find local teams near you.

Bay City Basketball

Bay City Basketball is dedicated to bringing San Francisco a high quality, professionally run basketball program. We can help you achieve your basketball goals and reach your maximum potential as a player through our camps and clinics, AAU basketball teams for boys and girls ages 8-18, youth basketball leagues and tournaments. Bay City Basketball is dedicated to bringing you a highly organized experience that is the right fit for you.

MRC Rebels Girls AAU Club

The MRC Rebels Basketball Club offers the highest level of skill development in the Bay Area. Our coaches are skilled trainers and offer year-round basketball training. We teach and reinforce proper basketball fundamentals, footwork, shooting, passing, rebounding, ball-handling, and defense, using the latest drills and techniques.

CYO Athletics

CYO Athletics in the San Francisco Archdiocese serves Marin, north coastal San Mateo and San Francisco counties and offers Boy’s and Girl’s Soccer, Boy’s & Girl’s Basketball, and countless other sports. Additionally we have a Physical Education program that we offer. Currently, we serve over 11,000 kids in 3 counties and continue to build leaders every day
