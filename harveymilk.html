Harvey Milk Photo Center

Visual Arts

The Harvey Milk Photo Center offers photography classes. You will learn photography techniques such as cityscape and intro to iPhone Photography. There are also events to attend showcasing work from other photographers.

Information

Join the largest darkroom in the community in San Francisco. This is the largest traditional darkroom in the country.
